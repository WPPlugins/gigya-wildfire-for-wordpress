This plugin integrate the Gigya Wildfire bookmarking service into your blog posts quickly and easily.

== Description ==

Allowing site visitors to quickly and easily share your content with their friends and acquaintances is paramount
to building traffic and your profile on the web.  Nothing makes that easier than Gigya Wildfire.

With the Gigya Wildfire for WordPress plugin installed you can choose to automatically append the Gigya Wildfire button
to the end of every post.  There are multiple options that let you choose where exactly the Wildfire button appears.

With minimal configuration needed, you'll be up and running in no time.

Now improved for better loading times!
